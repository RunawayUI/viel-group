About us

In 1995 we have found our first successful project, which set a strong intellectual and organizational impulse towards understanding and developing our strategy in future ventures. A year later, using the same approach, our second manufacturing facility was started, followed by several others in the coming years.

A well-thought-out systematization and complex application of new production and management technologies, innovative communications, equipment, development and materials, made it possible not only to create successful enterprises very quickly but also to open and lead the new market niches.

As a result, a system of organizing new businesses in diverse areas was created. This approach allowed us to actively and effectively diversify our business and to open new successful companies while expanding our expertise in various areas and directions.

growing vast

After over 25 years of practice, a team of experts has organically been formed, who are interested in setting more global goals and using increasingly innovative approaches for unique solutions.

In the recent as the number of new ideas, projects and technologies entering our sphere of interest began to significantly exceed our capabilities for their implementation, it was decided to move to a new stage of development.

Starting in 2019, our team, which previously provided solutions only for their own divisions, began to create complex solutions for third-party companies. This decision allows us to implement projects for international corporations and to create open mutually beneficial synergies with them.

We hope that our collective knowledge, practical experience and skills will be useful for dynamically growing companies with a positive outlook for the future.
